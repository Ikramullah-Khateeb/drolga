About Dr. Olga

Precision. Passion. Preservation.

Dr. Olga Vasylenko is a Specialist Endodontist dedicated to the art of saving teeth. While many rush to extract, Dr. Olga rushes to restore. Her approach is defined by her "Microscopic Philosophy." By using a dental operating microscope, she can identify and treat issues that are invisible to the naked eye, ensuring higher success rates for root canals and retreatment cases.

Her credentials span the globe, showcasing a distinguished academic and professional journey. She holds a Master’s Degree in Endodontics from Kharkiv National Medical University, a Diploma in Aesthetics from the University of Nancy in France, and is a resident of the prestigious Royal College of Edinburgh in the United Kingdom. She is also a proud member of the elite StyleItaliano community, representing the world’s highest standards in restorative dentistry.

Currently practicing in Dubai (GMCClinics) and Abu Dhabi, she combines her surgical precision with an artistic touch, ensuring that a "fixed" tooth looks as beautiful as a natural one.
